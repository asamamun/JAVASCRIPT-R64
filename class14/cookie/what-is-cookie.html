What are javascript cookies?

Normally web is stateless but cookies/web storage make the web site stateful

Cookies are small text files that are stored on the user's computer when they visit a website.

They are used to remember information about the user, such as their preferences, login status, language preferences, currency preferences and other settings.

They are also used to track and analyze website usage, such as page views and user behavior.
