Operators

  • Arithmetic operators: +,-,*,/,%,++,--
  • Assignment operators: =,+=,-=,*=,/=%
  • Comparison operators: ==,!=,>,<,>=,<= (result is always boolean)
  • Logical operators: &&,|| , !
  • Bitwise operators: &,|,^
  • Unary operators: ++,--
  • Special operators: instanceof, void, typeof, delete, new, in

Operands

In x = 5 + 6:
x, 5 and 6 are operands
= and + are operators
